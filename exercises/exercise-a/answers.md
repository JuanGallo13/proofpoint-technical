## 1. Describe the most relevant learning outcomes from programming-related courses throughout your degree.

The most important learning outcome from programming-related courses was not only learning specific programming languages, but developing problem-solving and system design skills.

During my studies I learned how to write clean and maintainable code by applying software design principles and best practices. I also developed experience working with Object-Oriented Programming (OOP), relational databases using tools such as MySQL and Transact-SQL (in personal projects), and frontend development, which was the role I mainly fulfilled during my thesis project.

These experiences helped me understand how to design software solutions that are efficient, maintainable, and focused on user needs.



## 2. Describe a situation you had to resolve with a classmate in a study or work group.

During a group project for an Artificial Intelligence course, our team had to build a multimodal image search system similar to Google Photos, where a user could input a prompt and retrieve relevant images from a dataset.

Our team had six members, but halfway through the project we noticed that only three of us were actively contributing. To address this, I proposed organizing an in-person meeting at my house so we could review the progress and work together.

Two of the less engaged members attended, and we were able to discuss the situation and redistribute the work. That meeting helped us make significant progress and nearly complete the project. The remaining member eventually dropped the course.

Organizing the meeting improved communication within the group and allowed us to finish the assignment successfully.

I am constantly motivated to learn new technologies, especially when I see how other developers use different tools to build systems or implement new features. This curiosity encourages me to keep expanding my technical knowledge.

In the short term, I would like to strengthen my skills in frontend development, database management, and programming with languages such as Python and Java, as well as gain experience with cloud platforms like AWS or Azure.
